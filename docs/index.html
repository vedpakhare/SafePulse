About SafePulse

SafePulse was created to make basic cybersecurity accessible to everyone. We believe that everyone deserves to know if their system has common security vulnerabilities.

Our mission is to provide free and secure cybersecurity tools for everyone, regardless of their technical knowledge.

Our vision is to become the pulse of safety in the digital world, helping users maintain healthy security practices.

For Kali Linux

# Download and install
